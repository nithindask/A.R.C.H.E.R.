What is A.R.C.H.E.R.?

A Really Calibrated Human Evaluation Recorder


A.R.C.H.E.R. is a precision-tier, rank-based, task-tracking system designed to monitor and enhance human effort across key domains: Study, Workout, Diet, Screentime, Initiative, and Discipline.


Your performance is quantified using a singular, divisible unit called the Arrow. Arrows are hard to earn but easy to lose, creating a system that rewards consistent effort and discipline.


Unlike gamified productivity apps, A.R.C.H.E.R. provides an objective framework for self-assessment without dopamine hijacks or artificial rewards.

How A.R.C.H.E.R. Works

1

Earn Arrows

Complete focused tasks to earn Arrows. 1 Arrow = 6 hours of study, 2 hours of intense workout, or 20 correct answers. Partial tasks earn proportional Arrow values.

2

Avoid Penalties

Missed workouts cost -2 Arrows. No study in a day costs -2 Arrows. Wrong answers deduct -0.2 Arrows each. Screentime beyond limits has escalating penalties.

3

Climb Ranks

Your total Arrows determine your rank from T (1-50) to Z (>2000). Each rank reflects increasing human capability and discipline.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I game the A.R.C.H.E.R. system? +

No. A.R.C.H.E.R. is designed with strict rules to prevent gaming. Arrows can only be earned through verifiable effort, and the system will deduct points for dishonest reporting. The scoring algorithms are designed to reward consistent, quality work over shortcuts.

Why isn't there a leaderboard or social component? +

A.R.C.H.E.R. is intentionally designed as a personal discipline framework, not a competitive game. The focus is on self-improvement rather than comparison with others. This prevents the dopamine-driven behaviors that undermine many productivity systems.

How does A.R.C.H.E.R. maintain memory of my progress? +

When using A.R.C.H.E.R. through ChatGPT, the system will maintain context within your current conversation. For persistent memory across sessions, you can periodically provide your current Arrow total when starting a new session, or use ChatGPT's memory features if available.

Can I modify the scoring rules for my personal use? +

While the core rules are designed to maintain system integrity, you can adjust certain parameters in your personal prompt copy. However, significant deviations may reduce the system's effectiveness as an objective measurement tool.
